## Operations
---
Sunrise Solstice
Located within Ashfall Mountain, Sunrise Solstice functions are a retreat of initiates as well as the headquarters for the Temple of Lux. 

All indoctrinated followers spend time at Sunrise Solstice to undergo the Rite of the Phoenix and find their true calling. Most followers find Sunrise Solstice to be their permanent residence after officially joining the temple.  They spend their days in worship, tending to food supplies, aiding in the education of children or undergoing temple approved education themselves, and preparing for the Day of Awakening.

Lost & Foundation
The Lost & Foundation is a charity organization founded by Rosemary. Their mission is to reunite families with their lost and missing children. Rosemary hosts a large gala every year to raise money for the group as well as help spread awareness of their goals.

Children of Lux
Also founded and run by Rosemary, the Children of Lux is an adoption agency that helps families find new homes for children when they can no longer provide them with adequate care. They will also assist women through pregnancy and birth for those unable to afford the necessary medical expenses as well as assist in the actual birth of the baby. Any unwanted babies that are born will be taken into the loving care of the agency and placed with a properly vetted family if a new family is not found in time. 

The Phoenix's Army
Overseen by Paprika and Oregano, the Phoenix's Army is an army of devoted followers, raised since birth, to aid the Phoenix on the Day of Awakening. They are train in all forms of combat to be ruthless killers. 

Oregano runs the combat and tactical divisions while Paprika specializes in espionage. Select soldiers will be chosen by her to be part of her spy division for covert missions.
